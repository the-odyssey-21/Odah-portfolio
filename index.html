Who Am I?

I'm a junior cybersecurity specialist with a passion for understanding how systems break. My focus areas include exploiting standalone systems, social engineering, and OSINT (Open Source Intelligence).

Beyond breaking things, I'm dedicated to building awareness around cybersecurity concepts. My mission is to inspire the next generation of ethical hackers and security researchers — because the best defense starts with understanding the offense.
